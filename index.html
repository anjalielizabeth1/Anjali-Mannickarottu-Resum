I am a second year biology major on the pre-medical track. Since a child, I have been looking forward to becoming a doctor. I would like to one day save lives ad alleviate human suffering starting with a few individuals then focusing on the community at large. I hope to contribute to medical research, disease/injury prevention, and public health initiatives. Creating meaningful and trusting relationships with patients is also important in the healthcare field as then are trusting us with their life and future. I am currently a volunteer at Riverside Community Hospital where we are assigned tasks like bathing, feeding and ambulating patients. Often, I sit with patients for hours just to speak with them or feed them. Some patients are not able to swallow properly, so I take that extra step and spoon feed any liquids such as water, orange juice or protein shake. If I was in their position, I would want someone looking over me and protecting my dignity. Patients are often sad or nervous in the hospital and it is our duty as healthcare workers to ensure their safety and comfort.
